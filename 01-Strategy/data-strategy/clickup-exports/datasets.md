## NPI
12 NPI symptoms into 4 distinct subsyndromes as follows: (1) psychosis (delusions \[Does the patient have false beliefs\], hallucinations\[subtypes: auditory vs visual\], and nighttime sleep disturbances); (2) affective symptoms (anxiety and depression/dyphoria); (3) hyperactivity (agitation/aggression \[stubborn and resistive to help from others\], irritability, euphoria/elation, aberrant motor behavior, and disinhibition); and (4) apathy (apathy and eating and appetite changes) \[\]
