**The single most important near-term action after AWS Jun 5.** The current draft (`ARPA-H_IGoR_Solution_Summary_REVISED_2026-06-02.md`) is framed Cytognosis-prime / Cytognosis-PI. It must be reframed to **IPAI (Purdue) prime, Cytognosis as funded sub-awardee** before submission.

**PI question (open decision):** Ananth as PI is cleanest now. Shahin as co-PI requires a PI-eligible Purdue Research Professor appointment. Decide with Ananth before Jun 9 Proposer Day.

**Scientific differentiator:** the **cellular micro-to-meso bridge** (single-cell genomic data, causal inference from genetic instruments, cell-type composition to thalamocortical circuit function). This is what distinguishes Cytognosis/IGoR from HSF: HSF = genomic + connectomic; we add the single-cell genomic-to-connectomic causal link. This framing must be central to the revised solution summary. Keep factorized-PRS confidential and off the bipolar paper.

Both IGoR and HSF are ARPA-H vehicles, but their scientific scopes are distinct and non-conflicting:

| | IGoR (TA1/TA2) | HSF / EVIDENT |
|---|---|---|
| Our scope | Mechanistic, multiscale, causal disease model: **molecular to single cell to circuit** (the cellular micro-to-meso bridge) | Genomic + connectomic; the HSF EVIDENT ISO is a broader mental-health mandate |
| Our differentiator | We add the **single-cell genomic-to-connectomic causal link** — the cellular micro-to-meso bridge — that HSF does not have | |
