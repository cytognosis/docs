- **Broad IPPIA / Manolis / PsychENCODE:** Shahin's "Associated Scientist" affiliation at Broad creates IP exposure for work done "during the term" or using Broad/Luria resources. Firewall strategy: Cytognosis platform work uses only independent data sources and Cytognosis compute; PEC/Luria access is scoped to the bipolar paper only; wind down Broad affiliation after the paper.

All three require Duane's guidance. The Manolis conflicts letter and Broad wind-down are the most time-sensitive.
