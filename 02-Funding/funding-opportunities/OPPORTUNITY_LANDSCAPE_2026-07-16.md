## 1. US Federal — ARPA-H Structure and Current Open Programs

ARPA-H organizes funding into four channels, all currently live per the official [Open Funding Opportunities page](https://arpa-h.gov/explore-funding/open-funding-opportunities) (verified 2026-07-16):

- **Programs** — time-bound, PM-led, ISO-gated. Currently open: **HEARING**, **IGoR**, **REST**.
- **Initiatives** — rapid proposal-to-contract tracks. Currently open: **TIGAR**, **BRAINS**.
- **Small Business Awards** — 2026 SBIR/STTR topics.
- **Mission Office ISOs** — rolling, accept ideas outside existing program/initiative scope, gated by a mandatory Solution Summary + written ARPA-H feedback before a full proposal is accepted. All four Mission Offices (Health Science Futures, Proactive Health, Resilient Systems, Scalable Solutions) are open on a rolling basis.

**Time-boxed initiatives that have since closed their main window** (context, not currently open as new-entry points): **EVIDENT** TA1–3 Solution Summaries closed Dec 22, 2025 (first teams announced Apr 21, 2026; **TA4 remains rolling**); **DELPHI** full proposals closed May 13, 2026; **RAPID** (rare-disease AI/ML diagnostics) closed Apr 2025; **PROSPR** remains in draft-ISO status (final not yet published as of this scan).

## 5. Academia-Industry Joint Schemes — Ali-Fundable Assessment

| Scheme | Mechanism | Verdict for funding Ali |
|---|---|---|
| **MSCA Postdoctoral Fellowships — Global Fellowship** | EU-national postdoc; 12-24mo outgoing phase at any non-EU host, 12mo mandatory EU return phase | **Best option identified.** Cytognosis could be the outgoing-phase host, drawing EC salary funding, if Ali (or a EU-national hire) has an EU return-phase partner lined up. Deadline 2026-09-09. |
| **MSCA Doctoral Networks** | Consortium trains PhD students via secondments across partner orgs | Partial — Cytognosis could host secondments (stipend-funded, time-boxed) as an associated/industrial partner, not a full salary line. |
| **Innovate UK KTP** | UK business + UK academic knowledge-base partner jointly employ an "Associate" | **Not usable as-is** — both required parties must be UK entities. Only a path if Cytognosis stands up a UK subsidiary or recruits a UK university as host. |
| **EIC Transition / Pathfinder** | Consortium moves research toward market | Not usable for Ali's salary — Cytognosis can only be an unfunded Associated Partner, not a paid consortium member. |
| **ANR-NSF CRCNS** | NSF-side award to the US institution in a bilateral French-US project | Usable — if Cytognosis is the US-side awardee, standard NSF grant funds can cover a US-based staff scientist. |

**Bottom line:** the MSCA Global Fellowship is the one EU mechanism that can put a salary on Cytognosis's books today without a UK/EU branch. Everything else in the "joint" category either requires Cytognosis to already have a UK/EU legal presence, or only offers unfunded-partner status.
