**[Cost Details]{.smallcaps}**

Proposers should provide the following cost details and include
supporting documentation that describes the method(s) and/or assumptions
used to estimate proposed costs. The government expects indirect costs
and profit to be included as part of cost proposal submissions. When
discussing labor costs, proposers should only refer to fully burdened
labor rates and identify and describe indirect rates only when they are
being applied to other cost elements requested herein.
