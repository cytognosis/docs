When submitting a proposal to ARPA-H, use of this template to describe
the planned technical tasks and subtasks is strongly encouraged. All
text in blue font may be deleted. There is no page limit to this
template.

Instructions:

1.  Provide the general objective of the proposal and a list of the
    tasks and subtasks that are being described.

2.  Within each of the tables, provide the information requested. Add
    tables as needed. Do not include proprietary information.

3.  For each task/subtask, provide:

- A general description of its objective.

- A detailed description of the approach to be taken to accomplish it.

- Identification of where it will be performed (e.g., at a commercial
  laboratory; a non-profit site; on the campus of an academic
  institution, etc.).

- Identification (by name) of the primary organization (prime
  contractor, sub-awardee(s), consultant(s)) responsible for its
  execution.

- Identification of all deliverables (e.g., data, reports, software) to
  be provided to the government in support of the proposed
  tasks/subtasks.

- Identification of whether it will involve human- and/or animal
  subjects research.

- Measurable milestones (e.g., a demonstration, event/activity that
  marks its progress or completion, etc.).

> Ensure the tasks and subtasks identified are also listed within the
> spreadsheets found under the tabs entitled "Ph \# Task \$" of the
> appropriate Cost Workbook. There is a spreadsheet for each Phase of
> this effort within each Workbook.
