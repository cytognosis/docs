# U01 — Objective (Astera 2026 overlay)

## U01.1 — Bold one-sentence thesis (300 char limit)

> We are building genotype-to-regulation foundation models that predict cell-type-specific gene regulation from genome sequence and reveal continuous, ontology-grounded disease axes underlying neuropsychiatric diagnoses, all on open data, code, and weights.

## U01.2 — Plain-language objective

We are training neural-network models that read raw DNA and predict, for each cell type in the human brain, which genes are turned on, by which regulatory elements, under disease conditions. Then we use those predictions on whole-genome sequences from 10,000+ donors to find continuous "disease axes" — quantitative coordinates that capture how schizophrenia, bipolar, autism, Alzheimer's, and other diagnoses share underlying biology rather than living in clean categorical buckets. Every model, every dataset, every line of code is released open.
