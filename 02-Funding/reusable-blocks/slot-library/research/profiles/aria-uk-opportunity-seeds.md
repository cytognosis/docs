# ARIA (UK) — Opportunity Seeds

## Eligibility
Same as ARIA Full Programmes: individuals (including unaffiliated), universities (incl. students/postdocs/staff), research institutions, SMEs and large companies, charities, public organisations. UK-based or willing to conduct >50% of project from UK. Foreign applicants permitted via UK partner.

## Funding range
£10k – £500k inclusive of VAT (where applicable) and all direct + indirect costs. ARIA funds 100% of costs — no match required. Inventory typical $250k. No minimum project length; maximum 3 years.

## Deadline pattern
Rolling. Continuous open submission for the rolling Opportunity Seeds call (latest version dated 28 January 2026 v1.0). Some opportunity-space-specific seed calls have discrete deadlines.

## Submission shape
Single-stage. **No concept paper.** Online application:
- 5 questions about the applicant + idea
- Proposal up to 3 pages
- Budget, IP, conflicts-of-interest details

## Required artifacts
- Online application form (5 questions)
- 3-page proposal
- Budget breakdown
- IP plan / declarations
- Conflict-of-interest disclosure
- Team CV / bio summary
- Letters of support (optional, situational)

## Required topics / questions
The 5 application questions probe:
1. What is the idea / objective in plain language
2. Why now / why ARIA / opportunity-space framing (where applicable)
3. Approach and key milestones
4. Team & relevant experience
5. Budget summary

## Schema gaps observed
- **3-page narrative cap** — render-time length constraint, no new slot needed.
- **100%-funded model** — funder metadata field "cost_share_required: false / 100% direct".
- **No-concept-paper variant** — funder file should support both staged and direct-submission shapes; F-family field "submission_stages" useful.
- **Unaffiliated-individual eligibility** — most other funders require entity. A03 sub-slot needed for "unaffiliated-individual eligible: true/false".

## Notes
- Lighter-weight version of ARIA Full Programmes for early-stage / proof-of-concept work.
- Some opportunity-seed calls are tied to a specific opportunity space (Programmable Plants, Scoping Our Planet, Forecasting AI).
- Rolling Opportunity Seeds call provides a "topic-agnostic" doorway.
- Strong Cytognosis fit for early-stage probes.
