# AstraZeneca CHANGE (Creating Health Access for Next Generation Equity)

> **Status**: Active

## Eligibility
- US-based 501(c)(3) nonprofits, public schools, government entities, municipal organizations that can accept tax-deductible charitable contributions
- 509(a)(3) designation NOT eligible
- Must serve communities in California, Delaware, Maryland, Massachusetts, or Texas (2026 cycle)
- Total revenue ≤$12M in most recent tax year
- Must improve access to quality healthcare for populations experiencing health disparities
- Services must consider social drivers of health

## Funding range
$50,000 – $500,000 typical (per fundsforNGOs aggregator). Multi-year possible. Grant amounts not published on AstraZeneca page directly.

## Deadline pattern
Annual cycle. Initial application by 2026-05-05 5pm ET. Finalists invited to full application 2026-06-10 to 2026-07-08 5pm ET via CyberGrants platform. Inventory deadline 2026-04-29 may be a different/earlier cycle phase.

## Submission shape
Two-stage:
1. **Initial application** (online) — short proposal
2. **Full application** (by invitation) via CyberGrants platform
