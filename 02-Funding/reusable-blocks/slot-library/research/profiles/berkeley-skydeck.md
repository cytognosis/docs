## Eligibility
- At least one team member must be UC-affiliated (UC student, alumni, faculty, staff, postdoc, visiting scholar — any UC campus or Berkeley Lab) OR qualify as a "global" founder.
- Multiple program tracks: Cohort (selective), Pad-13 (alumni), HotDesk (early-stage, broader)

## Funding range
- **Cohort**: $200k investment + ~$750k in-kind resources (legal, financial, tech)
- **HotDesk**: 60-80 teams, no cash investment; access to mentorship, advisors, $750k in-kind
- **Pad-13**: alumni track
Inventory $75,000 likely reflects HotDesk-tier in-kind value.

## Deadline pattern
Cohort batches (announced via skydeck.berkeley.edu/apply). Multiple application waves per year.
