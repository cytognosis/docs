# Breakthrough Energy Ventures (BEV)

## Eligibility
Climate technology companies. Any stage, any geography, any size. Hard criteria: technology must (1) potentially reduce ≥0.5 gigaton GHG/yr (~1% of 2050 global emissions), (2) be scientifically feasible at scale, (3) be capable of attracting additional investors.

## Funding range
Multi-stage (early to growth). Single checks $5-50M+ typical. Inventory typical $10M.

## Deadline pattern
Rolling. No formal application portal — typical VC process via warm intro + pitch.

## Submission shape
Direct outreach / warm intro. Pitch deck + diligence.

## Required artifacts
- Pitch deck
- Technical scientific summary
- Market sizing / commercialization path
- Cap table / fundraising history

## Required topics / questions
- Decarbonization potential (≥0.5 Gt/yr)
- Scientific feasibility at scale
- Affordability/reliability for global scale ("superior performance")
- 20-year timeframe acceptable
- Scalability across geographies/sectors
