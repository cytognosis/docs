## Eligibility
Early-stage social impact organizations: post-pilot, pre-scale (program/product/service already deployed with early impact signals). Founder must be full-time or intend to go full-time. Geographic focus: Africa, Europe, India, United States, occasionally Latin America. Both nonprofit and for-profit social enterprises eligible.

## Funding range
Up to $300,000 USD over 3 years, in multiple tranches. Either unrestricted grant funding OR investment capital (equity/SAFE/note) — instrument determined during closing process. **Plus deep capacity support**: portfolio management, board placement, fundraising introductions.

## Deadline pattern
Rolling. Applications accepted year-round.

## Submission shape
Online application (~30-60 minutes to complete):
- Executive summary, pitch deck, OR business plan (existing materials acceptable)
- Standard CV/resume for founder(s) and key leadership

## Required artifacts
- Online application form
- Executive summary OR pitch deck OR business plan (one of these required)
- CV/resume (text bios not accepted)
- Financial projections (optional)
- Theory of change / impact model
- Evidence of early impact (post-pilot, pre-scale signal)

## Required topics / questions
- **Problem-first**: urgent or critical social/environmental problem with innovative approach for underserved populations
- **Systems-thinking**: solution leverages existing systems, stakeholders, infrastructure, incentives
- **Scalability**: ability to scale exponentially, reaching ≥10,000 lives within 5 years
- **Team**: founder full-time commitment, leadership composition
- **Stage**: post-pilot, pre-scale evidence
- **Geography**: served regions
- **Theory of change**: causal model from program to impact
- **Sustainability**: revenue model, post-DRK plan
