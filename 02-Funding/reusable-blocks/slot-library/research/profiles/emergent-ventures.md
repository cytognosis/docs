# Emergent Ventures (Mercatus Center / Tyler Cowen)

> **Status**: Active

## Eligibility
All applicants 13+ years of age. Global. Individuals, teams, formal/informal projects. No entity required. Grants OR fellowships (fellowships involve in-residence time at Mercatus Center in Northern Virginia).

## Funding range
$1,000 – $50,000 typical for grants. Fellowships involve in-residence stipends. Decision usually within 2-3 weeks (guaranteed response).

## Deadline pattern
Rolling. Continuously open. No cycles.

## Submission shape
Online form (mercatus.tfaforms.net/5099527) — **3 questions only**:
1. Description of project / idea
2. Background (who you are, why you)
3. Specific use of funds requested

## Required artifacts
- Online form (3 questions)
- (Optional) link to portfolio / writing / past work
