# Fast Forward Accelerator (Tech Nonprofit)

> **Status**: Active
> **Date**: 2026-07-10
> **Author**: @shahin

> **Tags**: `funding`, `opportunity-profile`

## Eligibility
- Early-stage **tech nonprofits** with MVP (minimum viable product). Idea/research-only stage NOT eligible.
- Legally registered nonprofit in any country (501(c)(3) NOT required; non-US OK).
- Software-first solution (services with linear-headcount-growth NOT eligible).
- In-person attendance required at end-of-February and late-spring/early-summer events.
- Visionary founders with mission-driven technical leadership.

## Funding range
- $25K+ unrestricted funding
- Mentorship from tech + social impact leaders
- Ongoing support / community access
- Travel + lodging stipends for in-person events

## Deadline pattern
Annual cohort. Inventory deadline 2026-09-08 likely tied to 2026 cohort cycle.

## Submission shape
Online application via apply.ffwd.org/2026-accelerator. Form-based.

## Required artifacts
- Online application
- Founder bios / leadership team description
- MVP demo / link
- Mission narrative
- Tech-product-as-central-to-impact justification

## Required topics / questions
- Visionary founders / mission-tech leadership
- Founder proximity to / lived experience of problem
- Depth and scale of social problem
- MVP demonstration
- Software-first / non-linear-growth scaling story
- Reach and meaningful impact ambition
- Path to sustainable nonprofit-tech model
