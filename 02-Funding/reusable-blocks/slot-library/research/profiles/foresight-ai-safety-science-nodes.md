# Foresight Institute — AI for Science & Safety Nodes

## Eligibility
Researchers and builders working on AI-powered science or AI safety. Foresight prioritizes projects that want to be active members of the Nodes hubs (San Francisco, Berlin, opening April 1 2026). Funding-only applications considered only in exceptional cases.

## Funding range
~$3M total annually distributed; per-grant $10,000 – $100,000 typical. Higher amounts toward AI safety, smaller toward longevity biotech / molecular nanotech.

Plus: office space at SF or Berlin Node, in-house local/private compute, programming.

## Deadline pattern
Application deadlines on the last day of each month (rolling-monthly).

## Submission shape
Online application:
- Project description
- Itemized budget
- Project plan
- Organizational documents
- Compute requirements (if any)
- Connection to Foresight community statement
