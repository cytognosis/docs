# Google for Startups Cloud Program — AI Tier

## Eligibility
- Use or plan to use Vertex AI or Gemini as foundation of primary product
- Have received qualifying VC funding from seed to Series A
- Standard Scale Tier (non-AI): Series A or below, ≤5 years old typically

## Funding range
**AI Tier**:
- Year 1: 100% Cloud usage covered up to $250,000 (Scale tier credits + $150k AI Tier addition)
- Year 2: 20% covered up to additional $100,000
- Total: up to $350,000 over 2 years
- Plus $10,000 for partner LLMs in Model Garden

**Scale Tier (non-AI)**:
- Up to $200,000 over 2 years (Year 1: $100k; Year 2: 20% / up to $100k)

## Deadline pattern
Rolling. 7-21 days to process applications.

## Submission shape
Online form via cloud.google.com/startup/ai.

## Required artifacts
- Online application
- Company info, stage, funding history
- AI use-case description
- Architecture / Vertex AI plan

## Required topics / questions
- AI as foundation of primary product
- VC funding stage
- Use case (Vertex AI, Gemini)
- Team
- Stage / runway
