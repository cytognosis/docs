# IndieBio / SOSV NY+SF (2026 rebrand: SOSV NY / SOSV SF)

## Eligibility
- Early-stage biotech / life-sciences startups
- Must be legally in California or New York during program (4-month residential)
- Should incorporate as US C-corp to attract investment
- Open to teams with prior funding (VC, angel, grants)
- Open to serial / veteran founders
- Global applicant pool

## Funding range
- $525,000 initial investment (was $250k or $500k historically)
- Plus follow-on (80% of capital deployed post-program)
- Equity-based (SAFE / equity)
- 4-month accelerator program

## Deadline pattern
Rolling. Two cohorts per year typically (Spring + Fall).

## Submission shape
Online application + interview(s):
- Application form
- Optionally pitch deck
- Interview (one or more)

## Required artifacts
- Online application
- Pitch deck (optional)
- Team CV
- Technical / scientific summary
- Market opportunity

## Required topics / questions
- Founder team
- Technical novelty / IP
- Market / commercial opportunity
- Stage of development (preclinical / wet-lab readiness)
- Capital plan

## Schema gaps observed
- **VC-as-funder structure** — equity/SAFE in exchange for funding. F-family field "instrument: equity | SAFE | convertible | grant | hybrid."
- **Residency / location-during-program** — A03 sub-slot for "in-residence-during-program location."
- **Incorporation requirement** — A03 sub-slot for "required incorporation jurisdiction."
