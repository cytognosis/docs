## Eligibility
Life-sciences companies (pharma, medtech, consumer health, biotech, diagnostics, digital health). No equity taken. Pay monthly license fee for space.

## Funding range
**No equity, no cash investment.** In-kind:
- Wet lab + dry desk space (varies by location)
- Equipment access
- Mentorship + entrepreneurial programs
- Industry connections / partner network
- Average 2-year residency
Inventory $200,000 likely reflects in-kind value annually.

## Deadline pattern
Rolling.

## Submission shape
Online application via jlabsresidency.smapply.io.

## Required artifacts
Non-confidential documentation:
- Background on science/technology + focus areas
- License and/or IP coverage
- Customer / target audience description
- Differentiation from competitors
- Team bios
- 12-18 month project plan with milestone map
- Funding sources and/or partnerships

## Required topics / questions
- Transformational science/technology
- Significant unmet medical or market need
- Strong team
- Financial solvency
- Alignment with J&J strategic areas of interest or adjacencies
- Project plan with milestones
