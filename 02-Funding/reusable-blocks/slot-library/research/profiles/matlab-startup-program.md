## Eligibility
Startups meeting all three:
- <5 years in business
- <15 engineers
- <$1M USD annual revenue
- VC/angel funding levels NOT a criterion

## Funding range
$0 cash. In-kind:
- MATLAB, Simulink, 100+ tools at startup pricing
- Application engineer + technical support (phone/email/online)
- Training in local languages
- MathWorks Online Training Suite (100+ hours, free)
- 50% off training credits
- Co-marketing opportunities
Multi-year program.
