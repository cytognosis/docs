## Eligibility
Early-stage life-science companies. **No UC Berkeley affiliation required.** Companies from all sectors of life science with need for specialized wet-lab operations. Capacity for ~40 companies. Global eligibility.

## Funding range
**Not a cash grant.** Bakar Bio Labs offers in-kind:
- Shared and private lab + office space
- Key equipment (fridges, freezers, centrifuges, cell culture rooms)
- Conference rooms, auditorium
- Core facility with sophisticated instruments
- Network access (mentors, investors, UC Berkeley faculty)
- $0 cash; rent-based model (subsidized below market)
