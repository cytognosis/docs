# Robert Wood Johnson Foundation (RWJF)

> **Status**: Active
> **Date**: 2026-07-10
> **Author**: @shahin
> **Audience**: leadership, grant team
> **Tags**: `funding`, `opportunity-profile`

## Eligibility
US-based applicants only. Preference for 501(c)(3) tax-exempt organizations (not private foundations or Type III supporting orgs). State/local government and academic institutions also eligible. Most opportunities US-only.

## Funding range
Per-program varies widely:
- Health Equity Scholars for Action: up to $260,000 per award (15 awards)
- Local Data for Equitable Communities: 30 grants × $50,000 (9-month period)
- Pioneering Idea Submission: rolling, exploratory
- Larger signature programs (Evidence for Action, Policies for Action): multi-million
RWJF F&A indirect typically up to ~25% MTDC (lower than NIH).

## Deadline pattern
Per-program. Some rolling (Pioneering Idea Submission, Exploring Equitable Futures). Most have annual cycles. Inventory deadline 2026-04-13 likely tied to a specific RFP.

## Submission shape
Per-program. Most use RWJF online application portal:
- LOI / pre-proposal first (some programs)
- Full proposal by invitation
- Each program has dedicated program officer for guidance

## Required artifacts
- Online application form
- Project narrative
- Budget + justification
- Letters of support / commitment
- Organizational financials
- IRS determination letter (501c3 verification)
- CVs / biosketches
- Timeline / workplan
- Equity / health-disparities framing (RWJF emphasizes)

## Required topics / questions
- Health equity framing (foundational to RWJF mission)
- Population/community focus
- Theory of change
- Methodology (research / policy / practice)
- Beneficiary / community engagement
- Anticipated outcomes
- Budget justification
- Sustainability / dissemination
- Workforce diversity (where applicable)

- Strong commitment to equity as a research priority area.
- Multiple signature research programs (Evidence for Action, Policies for Action, Health Data for Action, Interdisciplinary Research Leaders).
