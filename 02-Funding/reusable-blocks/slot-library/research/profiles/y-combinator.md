## Eligibility
- All companies eligible (for-profit; some nonprofit cohorts historically)
- Founders typically full-time
- Global eligibility; relocation to Bay Area required during 3-month program
- Any stage (idea-stage to post-revenue acceptable)

## Funding range
- $500,000 standard deal: $125k for 7% equity (post-money SAFE) + $375k uncapped MFN SAFE
- Plus YC Accelerator program (3 months, in-person San Francisco Bay Area)

## Deadline pattern
Two batches per year (Winter + Summer cohorts). Inventory deadline 2026-05-04.

## Submission shape
Online application via ycombinator.com/apply. ~38 questions covering:
- Founder background
- Company / product
- Market / users
- Funding history
- Compliance / disclosures

## Required artifacts
- Online application form (Q1-Q38)
- 1-minute video introduction
- Demo URL
- Founder LinkedIn / portfolio
- Pitch deck (optional, encouraged)
