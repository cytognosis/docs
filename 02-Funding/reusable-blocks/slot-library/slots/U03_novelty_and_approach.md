# U03 — Novelty & approach

## U03.1 — Core insight / thesis of novelty

Cytognosis treats disease as a geometry problem inside the cell. The core novelty is a three-part architecture — Cytoverse, Cytoscope, Cytonome — that together turn cellular state into an addressable, continuously-updated map rather than a sequence of episodic snapshots. Strategy and execution are coupled via a four-layer hybrid (Three Horizons, OGSM, Hoshin, OKRs), which keeps long-horizon research and near-term delivery on the same spine.

## U03.3 — Why it will work now (enabling conditions)

Three enabling conditions have converged: (1) single-cell and spatial multi-omics are now cheap and standardized enough to build longitudinal per-person baselines; (2) open biomedical knowledge graphs (LinkML, Biolink) make the Cytoverse a buildable artifact rather than a metaphor; (3) edge-first inference — running the Cytonome on the patient's own device — makes continuous monitoring compatible with privacy, latency, and cost constraints that previously made proactive care infeasible.
