## Project summary

Cytognosis builds Cytoverse, an open multiscale model of human biology (proving ground: precision psychiatry). We request Claude API credits to accelerate the AI-orchestration and knowledge-curation layers.

---

## How Claude will be used

1. **Agentic literature and biotype curation** across psychiatric and neurodegenerative disease — assembling the reproducible, well-cited biotype corpus that anchors the Cytoverse map.
2. **Experiment and analysis-design assistance** — uncertainty-driven "what to measure next" active-learning logic (the same orchestration layer as ARPA-H IGoR).
3. **Macro-scale language layer of the Cytonome navigator** — on-device Gemma for inference; Claude for development, evaluation, and distillation targets.
4. **Code generation** for the open pipeline (Apache 2.0 / CC BY).

---

## Significance

Open, reproducible infrastructure for precision psychiatry. Replaces categorical DSM labels with biology-anchored coordinates, enabling individualized treatment matching for the 53 million U.S. adults living with mental illness.
