## Apply for the Biswas Family Foundation Fast Grant

The Biswas Family Foundation Fast Grants are small, fast gifts to researchers who want to explore an idea at the intersection of AI/data and health — but lack the seed capital to try. We hear consistently that PIs cannot easily buy compute, model-training credits, dataset access, or coding-agent tokens through traditional grants. We're closing that gap.

  

No CVs, no letters of support, no full proposals, no figures. Decisions are made from your written application alone — there is no interview round. We may ask funded grantees for a short Zoom during the project to understand how things are going, but it is not part of the award process.

  

More details on the program, including what grants can cover, the types of projects we're looking to support, and other requirements, can be found [**here**](http://biswasfamilyfoundation.org/science/fast-grants).

  

Please reach out to fastgrants@biswasfamilyfoundation.org with any questions.
