# Career development and transition funding

Applications are open until further notice and will be assessed on a rolling basis.

  

A summary of the program is available [here](https://www.openphilanthropy.org/career-development-and-transition-funding/).

  

Please contact [careerdevelopmentfunding@coefficientgiving.org](mailto:careerdevelopmentfunding@coefficientgiving.org) with any questions.

  

Generally speaking, we aim to review proposals within 6 weeks of receiving them, although this may not prove possible for all applications. Candidates who require more timely decisions can indicate this in their application forms, and we may be able to expedite the decision process in such cases.

  

By default, we will share your application with staff within Coefficient Giving (previously Open Philanthropy) as well as a select number of external advisors. If there’s any information that you’d like to keep confidential to Coefficient Giving staff, please share it in the relevant form field below.

  

Update (May 2026): For the next several weeks, applications to this program will be evaluated jointly with [BlueDot Impact](https://bluedot.org/), which runs related career transition grant programs. Final funding decisions will continue to be made by Coefficient Giving.

  

The form should auto-save any information you enter, so if you aren't able to complete it in one session, you should be able to fill in parts of it and return to it at a later point (as long as you are using the same device). You will receive a copy of your responses via email.

  

When sharing Google Docs or Sheets, please grant comment access to cb-funding@coefficientgiving.org to facilitate efficient follow-up communication.
