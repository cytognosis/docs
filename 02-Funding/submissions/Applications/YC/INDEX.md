## Open decision: apply this batch or wait for more Yar progress

**Recommendation: apply for the Fall 2026 batch. Do not wait.**

- YC's bar is founder-market fit and a working MVP, not finished traction. The draft already
  states plainly that the product has no real users yet, which is an accepted stage for a
  consumer AI application at this batch, not a disqualifying gap.
- The last two rejections (Winter 2025, Spring 2026) pitched the nonprofit science platform,
  a structural mismatch for YC. The for-profit Yar/PBC pivot in this draft has never actually
  been tested with YC, since the Summer 2026 version was drafted but not submitted.
- There is no cost to applying beyond founder time, and the batch itself supplies the forcing
  function (cofounder search, first users, retention data by Demo Day) that the draft argues
  the product needs next.
- The Oct 1 runway cliff on the nonprofit side makes a parallel, non-dilutive-to-mission,
  high-upside path (YC) worth pursuing now rather than deferring another cycle.
- Flagged, not resolved: if no forward progress has occurred on Yar since the Summer 2026
  draft (no TestFlight, no design partners engaged), a few days spent securing even one
  concrete proof point before Jul 27 would strengthen the application. That is worth doing if
  time allows; it is not a reason to skip this batch.
