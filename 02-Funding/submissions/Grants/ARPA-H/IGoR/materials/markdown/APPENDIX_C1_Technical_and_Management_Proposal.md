# Appendix C.1: Technical and Management Proposal

> [!NOTE]
> **Type:** DOCX · **Source file:** `materials/APPENDIX+C.1_Technical+and+Management+Proposal_IGoR.docx` · **Extracted text:** `materials/extracted-text/APPENDIX_C.1_Technical_and_Management_Proposal_IGoR.txt`
> **Governs:** The 40-page Technical and Management Proposal volume; the primary evaluated document.

## At a glance (BLUF)

Appendix C.1 is the fill-in template for the main proposal narrative. It is capped at 40 pages (Sections 1 through 7; Gantt chart and biosketches are exempt). It covers everything from the cover-page cover table through commercialization strategy and team management, and it is evaluated first and most heavily by ARPA-H reviewers.

## Key facts

| Item | Requirement |
|---|---|
| **Total page limit** | 40 pages (Sections 1 through 7) |
| **Gantt chart** | Exempt from the 40-page limit |
| **Biographical information** | Exempt from the 40-page limit |
| **References cited** | Exempt from the 40-page limit |
| **Commercialization Strategy Narrative** | No more than 3 pages (within the 40) |
| **Capabilities/Management Plan** | No more than 2 pages (within the 40) |
| **Award instrument** | Other Transaction (pre-filled in template) |
| **Proposal validity** | Minimum 120 days |
| **NIH Biosketch format** | Acceptable for biographical information |
| **Organization types** | For-Profit, Non-profit, Academia (check all that apply) |

## Structure / contents

Cover page table (required fields):
- Proposal title
- Prime organization name and type
- SAM.gov UEI
- Contact address (state)
- Internal reference number (if any)
- Key personnel/staff (name, email, organization; repeat as needed)
- Prime Technical POC (name, address, telephone, email)
- Prime Administrative POC (name, address, telephone, email)
- Award instrument requested (Other Transaction)
- Animal subjects (Yes/No)
- Human subjects (Yes/No)
- Total funds requested from ARPA-H (all phases)
- Resource sharing (government and performer percentages or dollar amounts)
- Place(s) of performance
- Date of proposal submission
- Proposal validity period (minimum 120 days)

Sub-awardee and consultant team-member table (slots for four orgs; add rows as needed):
- Organization name, Technical POC (name, telephone, email), Organization type

Proposal sections (ordered; Sections 1 through 7 count toward the 40-page limit):

1. **Proposal Summary**
   - A. Overview (nine required narrative questions)
   - B. Innovative Claims Table (ISO reference, technical challenge, innovation/evidence, proposal reference)

2. **Goals and Impact**

3. **Gantt Chart Timeline** (exempt from page limit)

4. **Technical Plan**
   - Credible plan for project goals
   - Technical challenges and solutions
   - How the solution meets Program Level and TA-specific IGoR ISO requirements
   - Measurable milestones with quantitative criteria and time-relative schedule
   - Task structure consistent with the TDD

5. **Risk Mitigation / Contingency Plans and Alternative Approaches**

6. **Commercialization Strategy Narrative** (3-page cap)

7. **Capabilities/Management Plan** (2-page cap)
   - Team expertise summary including sub-awardees and consultants
   - Identified Principal Investigator (PI) with roles and responsibilities

8. **Biographical Information** (exempt from page limit; NIH Biosketch format acceptable)

9. **References Cited** (exempt from page limit; include DOI or PubMed ID where available)

## What it requires of a proposer

- **Complete the cover-page table** in full: proposal title, prime org, SAM.gov UEI, all POC fields, animal/human subjects checkboxes, total funds, resource sharing, place of performance, submission date, validity period.
- **List all sub-awardees and consultants** in the team-member table; add rows beyond the four provided slots as needed.
- **Answer all nine Proposal Summary overview questions** (see Section 1.A); omitting questions weakens conformance.
- **Populate the Innovative Claims Table** (Section 1.B) linking ISO references, technical challenges, and proposed innovations with evidence.
- **Provide a Gantt chart** covering all applicable TAs and Phases in Gantt format; it is outside the page limit but required.
- **Align the Technical Plan milestone schedule** exactly with the Task Description Document (TDD) task structure.
- **State milestones quantitatively** wherever possible (e.g., sensitivity/specificity thresholds, not just "assay validated").
- **Cap the Commercialization Strategy Narrative at 3 pages** within the 40-page limit.
- **Cap the Capabilities/Management Plan at 2 pages** within the 40-page limit.
- **Name a Principal Investigator** and clearly describe team organization, roles, and responsibilities.
- **Provide biosketches** for all key personnel; NIH Biosketch format is acceptable. These do not count against the 40-page limit.
- **Provide full references** with DOI or PubMed ID where available; these do not count against the 40-page limit.
- **Delete all blue instructional text** before submission.

## Watch-outs

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The 40-page cap applies to Sections 1 through 7 only. Gantt chart, biosketches, and references are exempt but still required.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The Commercialization Strategy Narrative has a hard 3-page sub-limit inside the 40 pages. The Capabilities/Management Plan has a hard 2-page sub-limit.

> [!WARNING]
> The task structure in the Technical Plan must be consistent with the Task Description Document (TDD). Inconsistencies between C.1 and C.2 can undermine technical merit scoring.

> [!WARNING]
> "Additional information not explicitly called for in the Technical and Management Proposal must not be submitted." Extra content may be included as bibliography links only.

> [!CAUTION]
> The template pre-fills "Other Transaction" as the award instrument. Do not change this.

> [!CAUTION]
> Resource sharing is optional but "may be evaluated in the context of overall technical merit, reasonableness, value, and potential public benefit." If resource sharing is offered, government and performer percentages must be stated explicitly on the cover page.
