# Appendix C: Proposal Format, Instructions, and Evaluation

> [!NOTE]
> **Type:** PDF · **Source file:** `materials/APPENDIX+C_Proposal+Format+and+Instructions_IGoR.pdf` · **Extracted text:** `materials/extracted-text/AppC_Proposal_Format.txt`
> **Governs:** All formatting, submission, and evaluation rules that apply to the full IGoR proposal package.

## At a glance (BLUF)

Appendix C is the master rulebook for IGoR proposal submissions. It sets the page limit (40 pages for the Technical and Management Proposal), defines five required submission volumes, and establishes four evaluation criteria in descending priority order. Non-conforming proposals may be rejected without review.

## Key facts

| Item | Requirement |
|---|---|
| **Technical and Management Proposal page limit** | 40 pages (Sections 1 through 7; Gantt chart exempt) |
| **Task Description Document (TDD) page limit** | No limit; does not count against the 40-page cap |
| **Cost Proposal and Workbook page limit** | No limit; does not count against the 40-page cap |
| **Admin National Policy Document page limit** | No limit; does not count against the 40-page cap |
| **Draft OT Agreement (redlined) page limit** | No limit; does not count against the 40-page cap |
| **Paper size** | 8.5 by 11 inches |
| **Margins** | 1 inch on all sides |
| **Font family** | Sans serif only; narrow or condensed fonts prohibited |
| **Font size** | No smaller than 11 point |
| **File formats accepted** | .pdf, .odx, .doc, .docx, .xls, .xlsx |
| **Language** | English only |
| **Proposal validity period** | Minimum 120 days |
| **Submission portal** | ARPA-H Solution Submission Portal |
| **Solution summary prerequisite** | Required before full proposal submission |
| **Coverage requirement** | Must address all four Technical Areas and all three Phases |

## Structure / contents

1. **Proposal Format and Instructions** (Section 1)
   - 1.1 Conforming submission criteria
   - 1.2 Notices (clarification requests, non-selection, selection)
   - 1.3 Proposal submission instructions
     - Submission matrix (five volumes with page limits)
     - Appendix E OT Agreement redline instructions

2. **Proposal Evaluation** (Section 2)
   - 2.1 Proposal review process
   - 2.2 Handling selection-sensitive information
   - 2.3 Evaluation and award disclaimers
   - 2.4 Review and selection process
   - 2.5 Evaluation criteria (four criteria, descending importance)
     - Criterion 1: Overall Scientific and Technical Merit
     - Criterion 2: Proposer's Capabilities and/or Related Experience
     - Criterion 3: Potential Contribution to ARPA-H Mission and User Experience
     - Criterion 4: Assessment of Proposed Cost/Price

3. **Policy Requirements and Miscellaneous** (Section 3)
   - 3.1 Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI)
   - 3.2 Intellectual Property
   - 3.3 Software Component Standards (open-source preferred; MIT/BSD/Apache 2.0 cited)
   - 3.4 Genomic Data Sharing (GDS) Policy (NIH NOT-OD-14-124)
   - 3.5 Organizational Conflicts of Interest (OCI)
   - 3.6 Organizational Agency Supplemental OCI Policy (SETA/advisory services restriction)
   - 3.7 Research Security Disclosures (NSPM-33; CHIPS Act Section 10631; MFTRP prohibition)
   - 3.8 Electronic Invoicing and Payments (Payment Management Services)
   - 3.9 Procurement of Synthetic Nucleic Acids or Benchtop Synthesizers
   - 3.10 Dangerous Gain-of-Function Research
   - 3.11 Government Furnished Property/Equipment/Information (none expected)
   - 3.12 Human Subjects Research (FWA, IRB, 45 CFR 46, 21 CFR 50/56 required)
   - 3.13 Vertebrate Animal Subjects Research (IACUC, AWA, VAS worksheet required)
   - Additional links and references

## What it requires of a proposer

- **Address all four Technical Areas and all three Phases** in the proposal; omitting any TA or Phase is a conforming-submission failure.
- **Submit a solution summary first.** Full proposals are accepted only after the solution summary step.
- **Format all pages** to 8.5 by 11 inches, 1-inch margins, sans-serif font no smaller than 11 point.
- **Include all five volumes:** Technical and Management Proposal (C.1), TDD (C.2), Cost Proposal (C.3), Admin National Policy Document (D), and redlined Draft OT Agreement (E).
- **Disclose and mitigate all OCIs** for the prime and all sub-awardees; include a written mitigation plan.
- **Certify no team member is party to a malign foreign talent recruitment program (MFTRP)** per CHIPS Act Section 10631.
- **Disclose any concurrent SETA/advisory services** to any ARPA-H office (current or within the past year).
- **Provide a good-faith IP representation** that the proposer owns or holds appropriate licenses for all IP used in the effort.
- **Prefer open-source licenses** (MIT, BSD, Apache 2.0) for software components; identify any gaps and plan for open-standard development.
- **Include human-subjects documentation** (FWA, IRB approval or plan) if the work involves HSR; no HSR costs may be incurred before all approvals are granted.
- **Include animal-subjects documentation** (IACUC approval or plan, AWA assurance, VAS worksheet) if the work involves ASR.
- **Register for electronic payment** through Payment Management Services (PMS) at pms.psc.gov.
- **Include a redlined copy of the Appendix E OT Agreement** with any proposed changes; silence is interpreted as acceptance of terms as-is.
- **Ensure proposal validity for at least 120 days** from the date of submission.

## Watch-outs

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The government will not consider pages beyond the 40-page limit for the Technical and Management Proposal. Pages in excess are simply not evaluated.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> A solution summary submission is required before the full proposal is accepted. Do not skip this step.

> [!WARNING]
> Proposals that do not address all four Technical Areas and all three Phases may be deemed non-conforming and rejected without review.

> [!WARNING]
> If the proposer (or any team member) is or was providing ISC/SETA or similar advisory services to any ARPA-H office within one calendar year of submission, that must be fully disclosed. Failure to disclose can remove the proposal from award consideration.

> [!WARNING]
> CHIPS Act Section 10631 prohibits participation by any covered individual in a Malign Foreign Talent Recruitment Program (MFTRP). Failure to disclose, or inability to mitigate, eliminates the proposal from consideration.

> [!WARNING]
> HHS funds may not be used for dangerous gain-of-function research or for life science research with entities in countries of concern (e.g., China), per E.O. 14292.

> [!CAUTION]
> Narrow or condensed fonts are prohibited, but the solicitation does not define those terms precisely. Use a standard sans-serif face (e.g., Arial, Calibri, Helvetica) at 11 point or larger to stay safe.

> [!CAUTION]
> The Appendix E OT Agreement redline does not count against page limits, but silence is interpreted as full acceptance. Review the draft agreement with counsel before submission.

> [!CAUTION]
> If HSR is proposed, "funding cannot be used toward HSR until ALL approvals are granted." This could delay Phase start if IRB approval is not obtained pre-award.
