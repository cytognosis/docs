# Appendix D: Administrative and National Policy Requirements Document

> [!NOTE]
> **Type:** DOCX · **Source file:** `materials/APPENDIX+D_Admin+National+Policy+Document_IGoR.docx` · **Extracted text:** `materials/extracted-text/APPENDIX_D_Admin_National_Policy_Document_IGoR.txt`
> **Governs:** All administrative certifications, disclosures, and compliance representations required to accompany the proposal package.

## At a glance (BLUF)

Appendix D is a mandatory compliance document that must be completed in full and submitted with every IGoR proposal. It collects team identification, OCI affirmations, research security disclosures, IP assertions, human and animal subjects certifications, tax/felony representations, and cybersecurity and biosecurity attestations. No page limit applies, and no portion of the document may be deleted (only blue instructional text may be removed).

## Key facts

| Item | Requirement |
|---|---|
| **Page limit** | None; does not count against the 40-page cap |
| **Format** | .pdf, .doc, or .docx; English only |
| **Paper / margins** | 8.5 by 11 inches; 1-inch margins |
| **Font size** | Sans-serif, no smaller than 11 point; 8 or 10 point permitted for figures, tables, and charts |
| **Deletions allowed** | Blue instructional text only; all other content must remain |
| **Proposal validity minimum** | 120 days (confirmed on cover page) |
| **Common Forms required** | Biographical Sketch, Current and Pending Support, Collaborators and Other Affiliations (COA) for every Senior/Key Person |
| **SciENcv** | Recommended for generating BioSketch and Current and Pending Other Support Form |
| **BioSketch attestation** | Must be signed (or digitally signed) |
| **All affiliations** | Must appear in the BioSketch, including unpaid positions throughout career |

## Structure / contents

Cover page table:
- Proposal title
- Proposer organization
- Technical POC (name, address, telephone, email)
- Administrative POC (name, address, telephone, email)
- Date of proposal submission (MM/DD/YYYY)
- Proposal validity period (minimum 120 days)

Main sections:

1. **Team Member Identification**
   - Organization name, U.S./non-U.S. status, and role for all team members (prime, sub, consultants, vendors of critical components)
   - Senior/Key Personnel table with confirmation that Biographical Sketch, Current and Pending Support, and COA are attached for each person

2. **Federally Funded Research and Development Center (FFRDC) Participation**
   - Yes/No disclosure; if yes, justification and evidence of no alternative source required

3. **Organizational Conflict of Interest (OCI) Affirmations and Disclosure**
   - 3.a: Current ISC/SETA or similar support to ARPA-H (Yes/No)
   - 3.b: Past ISC/SETA support within one calendar year (Yes/No)
   - 3.c: Other potential OCIs (Yes/No)
   - If yes to any: separate document with ARPA-H office name, contract number, team member name/type, and OCI mitigation plan

4. **Research Security Disclosure**
   - 4.a: Certification that all PIs and senior/key persons have certified MFTRP status
   - 4.b: Any team member participation in Foreign Talent Recruitment Programs (Yes/No; detail if yes)
   - 4.c: Foreign ownership of any proposing entity (Yes/No; detail if yes)
   - 4.d: Research to be executed within a Foreign Country of Concern (FCOC) (Yes/No; detail if yes)
   - 4.e: Reliance on technology, data, or equipment from or transmitting to an FCOC (Yes/No; detail if yes)
   - Submission constitutes certification of accuracy and completeness; misrepresentation is prosecutable under 18 U.S.C. 287/1001/1031 and 31 U.S.C. 3729-3733/3802

5. **Novelty of Proposed Work**
   - Disclosure of any other government solicitations to which the proposed work has been submitted; funding status

6. **Intellectual Property (IP)**
   - Background IP and Data Assertions table (general description, role in performance, basis for assertion, associated rights, asserting entity)
   - Rights categories: Unlimited Rights, Government Purpose Rights, Limited Rights, Specially Negotiated/Other Rights
   - 6.4: ARPA-H generally expects license rights commensurate with its funding contribution
   - **Patents subsection (7):** documentation of ownership or licensing rights; for unpublished applications, patent number, inventor names, assignee, filing date, provisional filing date, and title summary required

7. **Human Subjects Research (HSR)**
   - Yes/No disclosure
   - If yes: IRB review documentation or plan; Federal-Wide Assurance number; table of organizations performing HSR with FWA numbers and IRB protocol approval status

8. **Animal Subjects Research (ASR)**
   - Yes/No disclosure
   - If yes: IACUC review plan; Vertebrate Animal Section (VAS) worksheet (olaw.nih.gov); Animal Welfare Assurance documentation; table of organizations performing ASR with IACUC and VAS status

9. **Representations Regarding Unpaid Delinquent Tax Liability or a Felony Conviction**
   - 9.a: Unpaid assessed federal tax liability (Is / Is not)
   - 9.b: Felony criminal conviction under federal law within the preceding 24 months (Is / Is not)

10. **Cybersecurity**
    - 10.a: Whether the organization implements a cybersecurity program with specific standards; description of incident response and security posture assessment
    - 10.b: Whether the organization has experience managing Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI); description of access controls, storage, and risk mitigation

11. **Biosecurity**
    - 11.a: Dangerous gain-of-function research involvement (Yes/No; per E.O. 14292, Section 8)
    - 11.b: Procurement of synthetic nucleic acids or benchtop nucleic acid synthesis equipment (Yes/No; if yes, OSTP Framework attestation required)

## What it requires of a proposer

- **Complete every section in full.** The document states it "must be completed in full"; no section may be deleted.
- **Attach Common Forms for every Senior/Key Person:** Biographical Sketch (signed), Current and Pending Other Support (signed), and Collaborators and Other Affiliations (COA). Use SciENcv for generation.
- **Include all affiliations in the BioSketch,** including unpaid positions throughout career.
- **Disclose all OCI situations** (current, prior-year, and any other) and attach mitigation plans for each. If none, check "No" explicitly.
- **Certify MFTRP status** for every PI and senior/key person; document any prior FTRP participation with names, years, FCOCs, and terms.
- **Disclose foreign ownership** of any team entity, with ownership percentages.
- **Complete the Background IP and Data Assertions table** for all background IP relevant to the proposed work; assign appropriate rights categories.
- **Provide patent documentation** for any patents or unpublished applications listed in the IP table.
- **Disclose HSR and ASR at the org/team level** in the tables; include FWA numbers, IRB protocol status, IACUC status, VAS worksheet, and Animal Welfare Assurance numbers.
- **Make tax and felony representations** (Sections 9.a and 9.b) on behalf of the corporation.
- **Describe the cybersecurity program** if one exists; address CUI handling specifically.
- **Answer the biosecurity questions** and include the OSTP Framework attestation if synthetic nucleic acids or benchtop synthesizers are in scope.
- **Confirm proposal validity of at least 120 days** on the cover page.

## Watch-outs

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Submitting Appendix D constitutes a legal certification of accuracy. Misrepresentations are subject to prosecution under federal statutes including 18 U.S.C. 1001 (false statements) and 31 U.S.C. 3729 (False Claims Act).

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Common Forms (BioSketch, Current and Pending Support, COA) are required for every Senior/Key Person. Missing or unsigned forms are a hard gate; ARPA-H can reject proposals for incomplete research security disclosures.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The biosecurity check is a hard compliance gate. Any dangerous gain-of-function research involvement (as defined in E.O. 14292, Section 8) disqualifies HHS funding.

> [!WARNING]
> OCI disclosure covers past support within one calendar year. If any team member has provided ISC/SETA-type support to ARPA-H in the preceding 12 months, that must be disclosed with full contract details and a mitigation plan.

> [!WARNING]
> Foreign ownership, FCOC-based research, or FCOC data reliance all trigger narrative explanations and may trigger additional RSR scrutiny. These are not disqualifying on their own but must be disclosed completely.

> [!WARNING]
> The tax and felony representations (Section 9) apply to corporations. Non-corporate entities (e.g., 501(c)(3) nonprofits) should confirm with counsel whether these representations apply and how to respond accurately.

> [!WARNING]
> Background IP with less than Unlimited Rights must be asserted in the IP table with a rights category. Silence is not equivalent to claiming Unlimited Rights and creates negotiation risk.

> [!CAUTION]
> The Novelty of Proposed Work section requires disclosure of any other government solicitation to which the same work has been submitted. If funding has already been received or a positive funding decision made elsewhere, that is a conforming-submission disqualifier per Appendix C Section 1.1.

> [!CAUTION]
> If HSR is involved, the IRB approval must precede any HSR expenditures. This creates a sequencing risk for Phase 1 start if IRB approval is pending at the time of award.

> [!CAUTION]
> The COA document template is available at an NSF link (https://www.nsf.gov/funding/senior-personnel-documents) that may change. Verify the current URL before submission.
