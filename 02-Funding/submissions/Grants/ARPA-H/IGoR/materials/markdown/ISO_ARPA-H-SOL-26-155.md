## At a glance (BLUF)

This is the master Innovative Solutions Opening document for ARPA-H-SOL-26-155. It establishes who may submit, when, where, and how, and serves as the gating document for the two-step process: Solution Summary first, then Full Proposal. Proposers must read this alongside Appendix A (technical requirements) and Appendix C (proposal format and evaluation criteria).

## What it requires of a proposer

- **Register in SAM.gov** with a valid UEI before submitting the Solution Summary. Registration must remain active through award. Allow 7-10 business days for new registrations; do not wait until close to the deadline.
- **Submit a Solution Summary by 06/25/2026, 12:00 PM ET** via https://solutions.arpa-h.gov/Submit-Solution/. Submission is required before a Full Proposal.
- **Comply with font rules** in the Solution Summary: sans serif, minimum 11-point, no narrow/condensed fonts.
- **Limit Solution Summary to 5 pages** (cover pages and References Cited section excluded).
- **Delete blue-font placeholder content** from the Solution Summary template before submitting.
- **Submit Full Proposal by 08/06/2026, 12:00 PM (Noon) ET** via https://solutions.arpa-h.gov/Submit-Proposal/, regardless of Solution Summary feedback (though discouraged feedback should be given significant weight).
- **Mark all proprietary pages** with "Proprietary" on the cover and each page containing such information. Do not use "Confidential" (reserved for national security classification).
- **Register on the ARPA-H Solutions site** before the Solution Summary deadline; late submissions due solely to registration delays may not be considered.
- **Confirm eligibility**, not an FFRDC, government entity, or current ARPA-H support contractor. If proposing inclusion of an FFRDC sub-performer, document exhaustion of all other options.
- **Confirm foreign entity compliance**, if applicable, affirm compliance with NDAs, security regulations, export control laws, and CHIPS and Science Act restrictions.
- **Direct questions** to https://solutions.arpa-h.gov/Ask-A-Question/ (not to ISO contact directly, unless for FFRDC exception guidance).

## Watch-outs

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Solution Summary is required, not optional.** The ISO states: "Solution Summary submissions are required." Missing the 06/25/2026, 12:00 PM ET deadline closes the submission pathway.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **SAM.gov must be active at both submission and award.** A lapsed registration at either point is a disqualifying condition. New registrations average 7-10 business days; start immediately.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **"Encouraged" vs. "discouraged" feedback does not gate proposal submission**, proposers may submit a Full Proposal regardless, but the ISO explicitly states: "potential proposers are advised to give the government's decision significant weight when determining whether to invest time and resources in the development of a proposal."

> [!WARNING]
> **FFRDCs and government entities are excluded as prime or sub-performer.** The exception (unique capability not otherwise available) requires documented exhaustion of alternatives. Do not assume a national lab or government lab can participate without this documentation.

> [!WARNING]
> **Current ARPA-H support contractors are categorically ineligible.** Any individual or entity providing technical, professional, financial, or administrative support services to ARPA-H cannot receive an award from this ISO.

> [!WARNING]
> **"Confidential" marking must not be used** for proprietary business information. ARPA-H handles submissions under FOIA; use "Proprietary" to designate trade secrets and business-sensitive content.

> [!WARNING]
> **Late registrations may not be considered.** The ISO states: "late submissions resulting solely from delays with registration may not be considered." This is a no-cure risk; complete SAM.gov and Solutions site registration well before the Solution Summary deadline.

> [!CAUTION]
> **Proposers' Day date is T.B.D.** as of ISO issuance (May 5, 2026). Monitor the ARPA-H IGoR program webpage and IGoR@arpa-h.gov for the scheduled date. Attendance is voluntary but "highly encouraged."

> [!CAUTION]
> **Full Proposal page limit is not stated in the ISO.** It is governed by Appendix C. Read Appendix C before drafting to avoid exceeding limits that would affect review.

> [!CAUTION]
> **LLM-assisted review is disclosed.** ARPA-H uses LLMs to extract data elements, summarize, and generate text during Solution Summary review. Proprietary information in Solution Summaries is transmitted via secure API only and is not used to train public models. This is informational; no action required from proposers beyond proper proprietary marking.
