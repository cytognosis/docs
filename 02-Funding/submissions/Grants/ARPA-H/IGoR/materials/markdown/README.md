## Conventions

- **Amendment 01** applies to Appendix B and Appendix E only (re-extracted 2026-06-14). All other documents are the original release.
- **Authoritative phase structure** is Appendix A: Phase I 18 months, Phase II 18 months, Phase III 24 months (60 months total). The OT cover-page phase numbers are unfilled template placeholders.
- **Deadlines** are governed by the ISO: Solution Summary `06/25/2026, 12:00 PM ET`; full proposal `08/06/2026, 12:00 PM ET`.
