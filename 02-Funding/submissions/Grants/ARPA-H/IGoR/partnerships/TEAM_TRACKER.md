## Status legend and document convention

- **Confirmed:** committed; appears in all docs **without a tag**.
- **In discussion:** named candidate, not yet agreed; tag **(in discussion)**.
- **Recruiting:** open role, no person locked; tag **(recruiting)**.
- **Alternate:** backup; not shown in external docs.
- **Tier 1:** primary, most-likely member for a role (for example Anne for TA4, Anna for the architect).

Never imply a candidate is committed; never signal an empty roster.

**TA3 (interoperable protocols).** **SIFT (Dan Bryce) is the confirmed TA3 lead** (formally accepted 2026-06-18); Robert Goldman is advisory. Full assessment in `SIFT_capabilities_analysis.md`. **Triple Ring Technologies** is a complementary TA3 systems-and-compliance integrator (ISO 13485) and a confirmed ARPA-H awardee, useful for hardware packaging and government-contracting rigor, not the standards-semantics lead. **Transfyr** (Renee Wegrzyn): DECLINED 2026-06-18; not available.
