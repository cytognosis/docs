# Inbound teaming request: SPOC Biosciences (TA4)

**Received:** 2026-06-14, via the ARPA-H IGoR teaming page. **From:** Lydia Gushgari, PhD, Chief of Staff and Director of Technology Development, SPOC Biosciences, Scottsdale AZ (www.spoc.bio, 480-442-8743). **Requested role:** TA4 subcontractor, wet-lab validation assays.

## Fit assessment (internal)

> [!NOTE]
> **Orthogonal, optional TA4 add-on, not a core cellular lab.** SPOC is a **cell-free protein and antibody** platform (not cell culture), so it does not cover our core TA4 needs (iPSC-neuron transcriptomics, morphology, electrophysiology). It could add value for **protein-level validation**: confirming TA1-predicted protein-protein interactions, mutational scanning of variant effects on binding, and plasma or CSF proteomics biomarkers. Worth a short scoping call; lower priority than the cell-based labs; not a blocker.

- **Fits our model where:** PPI validation for TA1 causal edges; deep mutational scanning of the variant panel at amino-acid resolution; proteomic biomarker readouts.
- **Does not cover:** cellular phenotyping (imaging, scRNA-seq, electrophysiology), which is the Phase I phenotypic-triage core.
- **Next step:** a 30-minute call to scope whether a defined, bounded TA4 protein-validation task is worth including; keep as an optional add-on in the slate.

## Platform and assays (from their message)

- **Cell-free expression** of up to 1152 proteins (full-length, miniproteins, peptides) or antibodies (VHH, scFv, Fab, mAb) directly on chips or sensors; TA2/TA3 can define the protein or antibody list.
- **Instrumentation:** surface plasmon resonance (SPR; full binding kinetics: on-rate, off-rate, affinity, residence time, expression), Cryo-EM (structure), MALDI mass spectrometry (structure and binding).
- **Applications:** disease mechanisms (PPIs, mutation effects, complete mutational scanning), plasma proteomics (biomarkers, autoantibodies, PTM- and isoform-specific, inflammatory markers), drug discovery (hit validation, affinity maturation, epitope analysis, developability, off-target), preclinical and clinical specimen characterization.
