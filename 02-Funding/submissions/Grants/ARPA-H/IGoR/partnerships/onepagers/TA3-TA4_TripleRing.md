# IGoR teaming: Triple Ring Technologies and Cytognosis/IPAI, TA3/TA4 systems and integration

**Team PsychIGoR. IPAI (Purdue) prime, with Ananth Grama as PI; Cytognosis and IPAI lead the disease-model and AI core (TA1 and TA2). We are assembling the experimental and systems side of the team, and Triple Ring's engineering and ARPA-H program experience is a strong complement.**

## What we bring, and have already built

- **A causal, self-updating disease model (TA1)** for psychiatric disease, grounded in penetrant genetic forms (such as 22q11.2 deletion) modeled in iPSC cells.
- **A New Science Engine (TA2)** that designs the highest-value next experiment by interrogating the model.
- **A Phase I experiment ready to run** (a phenotypic-triage screen across transcriptomic, morphological, and functional calcium-imaging readouts), which must be packaged into portable, validated, instrument-level procedures.
- A committed prime (IPAI/Purdue) and a single-cell and psychiatric-genetics core.

## Where Triple Ring fits

- **TA3 systems integration and compliance.** Turning declarative protocols into validated, hardware-level procedures across heterogeneous instruments, with ISO 13485-grade documentation and quality processes, complementing the open-protocol (LabOP) standards layer.
- **TA4 instrumentation and integration.** Imaging, biochemical, and functional-assay system development, and the hardware and software integration that lets validated laboratories run the same experiment reproducibly.
- **ARPA-H program execution.** Your experience as an ARPA-H awardee (sub and multiparty) and your contracting and program-management muscle de-risk delivery for the whole team.

## Why this is a strong match

- Clean complementarity: **we bring the disease biology and AI; you bring systems engineering, instrumentation, and government-program rigor**, with no overlap.
- An ISO 13485-certified, ARPA-H-experienced integrator materially strengthens the reproducibility and execution story IGoR scores.
- Bay Area proximity makes a fast, in-person working relationship easy, and your incubator and co-development network may surface further capability.
