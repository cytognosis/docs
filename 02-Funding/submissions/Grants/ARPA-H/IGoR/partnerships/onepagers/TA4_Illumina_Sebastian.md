# IGoR teaming: Illumina and Cytognosis/IPAI, TA4 sequencing and data

**Team PsychIGoR. IPAI (Purdue) prime, with Ananth Grama as PI; Cytognosis and IPAI lead the disease-model and AI core (TA1 and TA2). We are assembling the validated-laboratory side (TA4) of an ARPA-H closed-loop program, and would value Illumina as the sequencing and data partner.** A short companion sheet explains the IGoR program itself.

## What we bring, and have already built

- **A causal, self-updating disease model (TA1)** for psychiatric disease, grounded in penetrant genetic forms (such as 22q11.2 deletion) modeled in iPSC cells.
- **A New Science Engine (TA2)** that designs the highest-value next experiment by interrogating the model, then ingests the results automatically.
- **A Phase I experiment ready to run:** a phenotypic-triage screen generating high volumes of single-cell sequencing across an isogenic iPSC variant panel.
- A committed prime (IPAI/Purdue) and a single-cell and psychiatric-genetics core; a team that already speaks Illumina's language end to end.

## Where Illumina fits

- **TA4 single-cell and bulk sequencing readout.** Every loop iteration produces cells that must be profiled fast; PIPseq (instrument-free single-cell) plus NovaSeq and DRAGEN is the **lowest-friction high-throughput readout** for an iterative perturbation program.
- **Model-ready data infrastructure.** Standardized, machine-readable outputs that flow straight back into the TA1 model, the data backbone of the marketplace (TA4).

## Why this is a strong match

- Illumina already has an **ARPA-H relationship** (the PCX pediatric-cancer program), so the path to a research collaboration on a government program is established, not net-new.
- The collaboration can start in whatever form fits Illumina best, **in-kind sequencing and reagents, an investigator-style research collaboration, or a named subcontract**; we are flexible on the vehicle and will help find the right internal owner.
- Strong existing rapport with Illumina scientists in the single-cell and neuroscience space gives us a credible, technically fluent working relationship from day one.
