# IGoR teaming: Operant BioPharma and Cytognosis/IPAI, TA4 iPSC-neuron laboratory

**IPAI (Purdue) prime, with Ananth Grama as PI; Cytognosis and IPAI lead the disease-model and AI core (TA1 and TA2). We are assembling the validated-laboratory side (TA4), and your iPSC-neuron closed-loop platform is an exceptional fit.**

## What we bring, and have already built

- **A causal, self-updating disease model (TA1)** for psychiatric disease, grounded in penetrant genetic forms (such as 22q11.2 deletion and high-odds-ratio SCHEMA genes) modeled in iPSC cells.
- **A New Science Engine (TA2)** that interrogates the model itself to design the highest-value next experiment, the AI side of a closed loop that your platform is built to close.
- **A Phase I experiment ready to run:** a phenotypic-triage screen on isogenic iPSC pairs across **transcriptomic, morphological, and electrophysiological** readouts.
- A committed prime (IPAI/Purdue) and a single-cell and psychiatric-genetics core, plus Cell Painting phenomics (Anne Carpenter) already on the team.

## Where Operant fits

- **TA4 iPSC-neuron laboratory and live-cell readout.** Your GEDI biosensors and robotic live-cell microscopy on human iPSC-derived neurons give us **longitudinal, single-cell, functional phenotyping**, including the electrophysiology-adjacent and live-imaging readouts central to our triage screen.
- **Closed-loop execution node.** Your closed-loop experiment-design and optogenetic control experience makes you a natural TA4 site for iterative perturb-measure-update cycles driven by our TA2 engine.

## Why this is a strong match

- You are, as far as we have found, **the only group combining iPSC neurons, closed-loop AI experiment design, and optogenetic control at scale**, exactly the neuro capability IGoR's loop needs.
- The Finkbeiner-lineage iPSC-neurodegeneration pedigree transfers directly to our **psychiatric** iPSC models; we bring the disease biology (schizophrenia, 22q11.2) and the genetics, you bring the live-cell functional platform.
- Clean division of labor: **we own TA1 and TA2 modeling; you anchor a TA4 iPSC-neuron site**, so the roles are complementary, not overlapping.
