I am leading the disease-model and AI core (TA1 and TA2) of an ARPA-H IGoR proposal, with IPAI at Purdue as prime and Ananth Grama as PI. Our team is PsychIGoR. That is where Illumina fits as a TA4 partner: high-throughput single-cell readout (PIPseq) and DRAGEN, plus standardized, model-ready data flowing straight back into the model.
