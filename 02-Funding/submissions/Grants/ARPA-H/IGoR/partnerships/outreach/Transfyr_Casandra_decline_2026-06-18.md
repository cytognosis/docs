Thank you for taking the time to meet and walk us through Transfyr. The execution-observability work you are building is impressive, and I expect it will prove valuable across a lot of programs.

After discussing internally, we have concluded it is not the right fit for this particular IGoR proposal. I would genuinely like to stay in touch as both efforts evolve, there may well be a better-fitting opportunity ahead. Thank you again, and best of luck with the program.
