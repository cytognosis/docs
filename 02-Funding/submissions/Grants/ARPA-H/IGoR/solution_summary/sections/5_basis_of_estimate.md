**Value to the U.S. taxpayer.** The deliverables are open: a self-updating causal disease model, an experiment-design engine, open protocol standards, and a validated-laboratory loop, all deposited in a public repository. Because the targets it surfaces carry human-genetic support, they are roughly two to three times more likely to reach the clinic, and the same platform generalizes across psychiatric and, ultimately, other complex diseases, multiplying the return on a single investment.
