## The reframe

Existing frames: **PRS as a blended risk number**, or **credible sets as individual targets**. Neither yields a mechanistic model, and neither respects that patients within a DSM label are heterogeneous while patients across labels share biology.

Our thesis: **disease-associated genetic variation is the causal perturbation operator** on a latent causal model of cellular biology, in the identifiability regime for soft interventions (Zhang et al. 2023; SAMS-VAE 2023). This inverts the virtual-cell paradigm (Bunne 2024) and makes the model mechanistic, multiscale, and clinically grounded.

## The map, four properties

1. **Continuous dimensional axes.** Monotonic, disentangled, healthy-anchored (Surreal-GAN lineage: Nat Med 2024 aging DNEs, Nat Biomed Eng 2025 nine DNEs, Nat Med 2026 MULTI Consortium).
2. **Transdiagnostic.** Aligned with Grotzinger 2025 five-factor scaffold, RDoC, HiTOP.
3. **Cell-type-aware.** PsychENCODE brainSCOPE, PsychAD, Ruzicka-Mohammadi 2024 *Science* atlas.
4. **Self-updating.** Ontology-conditioned closed loop; sub-24-hour update latency by Phase II. **Not** a large-language-model wrapper.

**Held confidential (not shared in outreach).** A factorization method that decomposes patient polygenic variation into sparse pathway-disentangled axes, and a related perturbation-modeling method under anonymous review, are IP-protected. Discussable under CDA.

## The penetrant anchor: 22q11.2 and TBX1

Idiopathic schizophrenia is polygenic and hard in a dish. We anchor first on **near-Mendelian handles**, exactly the move behind APP/PSEN1 (Alzheimer) and LRRK2/SNCA (Parkinson).

| Handle | Evidence |
|---|---|
| **22q11.2 deletion** | ~25x psychosis risk; 11.5% pooled psychotic-disorder prevalence (Schneider 2023); spans PRODH, DGCR8, COMT, TBX1, GNB1L |
| **TBX1 (used carefully)** | Tbx1+/- reduced PPI (Paylor 2006); corticogenesis (Vitelli 2017); oligodendrocyte myelination (Kim-Bhatt 2025). Framed as **high-penetrance developmental handle within 22q11DS**, not standalone schizophrenia gene (Funke 2007 caveat stated) |
| **High-OR SCHEMA** | SETD1A, CUL1, XPO7, GRIN2A, GRIA3, CACNA1G, TRIO, SP4, HERC1, RB1CC1 |

Phase 1 anchor experiment: **isogenic iPSC pairs** (CRISPR variants on common healthy background), phenotypic-triage screen across the panel (scRNA-seq + Cell Painting + calcium imaging). Extends Tegtmeyer / Carpenter 2025 NeuroPainting from one deletion to a multi-variant, multi-modal screen.
