# For Convergent Research (Adam Marblestone): a map-only FRO for psychiatric disease biology

**BLUF.** Inside our $42.9M ARPA-H IGoR program sits a **cleanly separable, FRO-shaped artifact**: a **universal dimensional map of psychiatric disease biology**, released openly. We think it fits Convergent's criteria and would like a 30-minute exploratory conversation.

**From.** Shahin Mohammadi, PhD. Founder & CEO, Cytognosis Foundation (501(c)(3)). TA1/TA2 sub-lead, ARPA-H IGoR / PsychIGoR. mohammadi@cytognosis.org.

## Why a nonprofit / FRO in the first place

Cytognosis is 501(c)(3) on purpose. The artifact we most want to build is **infrastructure, not a product**. Downstream value is diffuse: academic PIs, biotech target-discovery, ARPA-H (EVIDENT, IGoR, Proactive Health), the VA, FDA expedited-approval sponsors, precision-psychiatry programs. No company captures enough of it to justify the build, and any company that did would own it, which defeats the point.

## The artifact, in one paragraph

A **continuous, dimensional, transdiagnostic coordinate system** for psychiatric disease. Mechanistically grounded in:

- **Molecular biotypes**: BDNF-plasticity, PV / gamma / E-I, oxidative-stress / mitochondrial, hyperdopaminergic, inflammatory, serotonergic.
- **Cellular biotypes**: PsychENCODE brainSCOPE, PsychAD, Ruzicka-Mohammadi *Science* 2024 schizophrenia atlas.
- **Circuit biotypes**: Drysdale, B-SNIP, Williams six-biotype, Beam-Etkin, Quah RDoC-imaging.
- **Cross-disorder genomic scaffold**: Grotzinger 2025 five-factor.

Anchored in **penetrant, near-Mendelian handles** (22q11.2 deletion, high-OR SCHEMA genes) in the tradition that made neurodegeneration cellular biology tractable (APP/PSEN1, LRRK2/SNCA). Generalizes to idiopathic and transdiagnostic psychiatry through learned axes. **Released openly under permissive licenses and FAIR-data terms.**

## FRO shape test

| Convergent criterion | Fit |
|---|---|
| **Concrete, shipping artifact** | Versioned coordinate system with defined interfaces (data schemas, ontology bindings, model APIs); Apache 2.0 code, CC BY docs, controlled-access DUAs where cohorts require. **v1.0 is "done."** |
| **Infrastructure with diffuse downstream value** | Academic PIs, biotech, ARPA-H programs, FDA sponsors, VA pilots. Not capturable by any one downstream. |
| **Neither grant nor company** | Coordinated multi-team engineering. Does not decompose into PI grants; no company would build it and open-source it. |
| **Time-bounded, dissolution-oriented** | 3-5 year build; at v1.0, custodianship transfers to an open-science steward (ENCODE / PsychENCODE precedent). |
| **Ships, does not perpetuate** | The FRO ships the map. Downstream orgs, including Cytognosis for wearables (Cytoscope) and on-device AI (Cytonome), figure out how to use it. |

## The split, and why disease biology is not IP-protected

- **Map only (FRO-shaped, open, Phase 1 anchor).** The coordinate system, the cell-type atlases, the penetrant-form iPSC phenotype library, the ontology, the causal-inference framework treating genotype as soft intervention. **Disease biology; US law makes this non-patentable, and we have no interest in trying.**
- **Kept confidential (not FRO scope).** A factorization method for patient polygenic variation, and a related perturbation-modeling method under anonymous review. **These are methods for using the map, not the map itself**; they can be licensed separately without affecting the map's openness.

**Principle: the substrate is a public good; specific methods for exploiting it can be IP where the downstream economics justify.** GRCh38 and ENCODE are open; specific variant-calling / interpretation methods over them are proprietary. Same pattern.

## Direct precedents from our own record

- **Ruzicka, Mohammadi et al. 2024 (*Science*).** Multi-cohort schizophrenia single-cell atlas across 388 brains.
- **Mathys, Mohammadi et al. 2019 (*Nature*).** ROSMAP Alzheimer single-cell atlas.
- **Tegtmeyer et al. 2025 (*Nat Commun*).** NeuroPainting 22q11 in iPSC-derived neurons + astrocytes; Anne Carpenter senior author. Phase 1 extends this to a multi-variant, multi-modal panel.
- **Grotzinger 2025 (*Science*).** Cross-disorder five-factor genomic scaffold, the reference axis system.
- **Surreal-GAN, DNE, MULTI Consortium.** Continuous, genetically-validated, healthy-anchored dimensional axes at population scale.
