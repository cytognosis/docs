# Video Storyboard

## Shot 1: Opening Slide/Text

Screen: Title text, "Yar: a local-first cognitive companion, built by and for neurodivergent minds."
Say: "Yar turns scattered thoughts and voice notes into a private, typed personal knowledge graph, and it is fully free."

## Shot 2: Desktop App, Home/Today

Screen: The Tauri desktop app, Today screen, morning check-in card.
Say: "This is the real desktop app: React and TypeScript inside a Rust shell, running fully offline by default. No accounts, no cloud."

## Shot 3: Capture

Screen: Capture screen; type or record a stray thought.
Say: "Capture is a private space first. Nothing gets sorted or categorized until the person chooses to."

## Shot 4: Task and Plan

Screen: Turn the capture into a task; show it land on the Plan screen with a lighter baseline version alongside the ideal one.
Say: "Plans carry a built-in lighter version. Doing the baseline still counts."

## Shot 5: Focus and Mood

Screen: Start a Focus session; then the Mood & Energy check-in and 14-day timeline.
Say: "The focus companion never punishes stopping early. Mood is tracked as texture, in feelings-words, never a clinical score."

## Shot 6: Thought Map

Screen: Place a thought on the Map; create a link between two thoughts.
Say: "The person can review, repair, and link their own graph locally, before anything ever leaves the machine."

## Shot 7: Assistant

Screen: Ask the Assistant a grounded question; show the reply with cited sources.
Say: "The assistant only ever talks about what the person themselves captured, and always shows its sources."

## Shot 8: Sync and Convergence

Screen: Settings, connect the optional personal server, trigger a sync, show "Projections match."
Say: "Sync is opt-in and personal. It exchanges operations, never database files, and both sides verify they agree with a projection hash."

## Shot 9: Safety Refusal

Screen: Submit "Diagnose me, do I have ADHD" to the Assistant.
Say: "CAP-Lite blocks diagnosis, treatment, and crisis language before any model call, and points to real human support. Yar is for knowledge capture and daily planning, not clinical decisions."

## Shot 10: Closing

Screen: Title card, "Fully free. No subscription. Built by and for neurodivergent minds."
Say: "Yar is fully free, with no subscription, indefinitely. This submission is the shipped Tauri desktop build; mobile and a live voice loop are next."
