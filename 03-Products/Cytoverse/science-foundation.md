**If you only read one thing:** Cytoverse is the map; the science that defines the map's coordinates lives in the Neuroverse research layer. This page is the bridge: it links each Cytoverse scientific claim to the research doc that grounds it.

## The claim Cytoverse rests on

Psychiatric and neurodegenerative conditions share overlapping genetic, neural, and behavioral structure. A continuous, multimodal, individual-level coordinate system can represent that structure better than categorical boundaries. Neuroverse is the research program that builds and validates that coordinate system.

## Documented vs. forward-looking

The docs above are written and canonical. Three science-foundation threads named in program planning are **not yet written up** and are tracked as pending research syntheses, not claimed as complete:

- **BDNF axes** (a candidate molecular axis of the coordinate system).
- **Factorized-PRS** (polygenic risk factorized onto the phenotype axes).
- **Bipolar** as a worked biotype-recovery case (B-SNIP is the landing cohort; see the Level 2 validation row in the Neuroverse [README](../../05-Research/neuroverse/README.md)).

These are listed here so the gap is explicit. Do not cite them as existing analyses until the corresponding docs land in `05-Research/neuroverse`.
