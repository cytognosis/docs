### Indication groupings

- **Antidepressant** → Drugs for depression (TCA, MAOI, SSRI, SNRI)
- **Stimulants**
- **Antipsychotic** (Neuroleptics, Major tranquillisers) → Drugs for psychosis (Typical/1st generation, Atypical/2nd generation)
- **Anxiolytic** → Drugs for Anxiety
- **Hypnotic** → Drugs for Insomnia
- **Mood Stabilizers** → Drugs for relapse prevention
