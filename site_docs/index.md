# Cytognosis Documentation

Cytognosis Foundation builds biomedical AI infrastructure that is **open, rigorous, and built for people** — from scientists running multi-omics pipelines to clinicians making decisions at the point of care.

This site documents the tools, standards, and frameworks we use and publish. Whether you are integrating the cytoskills toolchain, reading the Cytoplex protocol specification, or learning the Cytoverse platform, start here.

## Key projects

**Cytoskills** — an AI skill and package ecosystem for the Cytognosis agent toolchain. Includes the `@cytognosis/cyto-skills` core library, CLI, and deploy packages, plus a library of branded skills for design, documentation, development, and orchestration.

**Cytoverse** — the integrated data and tooling platform: asset management, environments, containers, and services, unified across the Cytognosis monorepo.

**Cytoplex (Cytonome)** — the Cytognosis Annotation Protocol (CAP), a federated standard for structured biomedical evidence linking claims, evidence, and provenance.

## About Cytognosis Foundation

Cytognosis Foundation is a 501(c)(3) biomedical AI nonprofit. We develop open infrastructure, rigorous standards, and compassionate tools to accelerate the understanding and treatment of complex disease.

Our work is grounded in 20 years of computational biology, spanning large-scale multi-omics, knowledge graphs, and human-centered AI design.
